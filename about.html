About Us – Havenridge Youth Centre

Welcome to Havenridge Youth Centre, a vibrant and inclusive space dedicated to empowering young people aged 12–24. We believe every young person deserves a safe, supportive environment to grow, learn, and connect.

At Havenridge, we offer a range of programs designed to inspire and uplift, from creative workshops and career development sessions to mental health support and sports activities. Whether you're looking for a place to unwind, meet new friends, or gain valuable life skills, our doors are always open. More than just a centre—we are a community. A place where ideas are nurtured, talents are discovered, and dreams take flight. Join us and be part of something great!

Our Mission

At Havenridge Youth Centre, our mission is to empower and uplift young people by providing a safe, inclusive, and inspiring space for growth, learning, and connection.

Through mentorship, skill-building programs, and community engagement, we strive to equip youth with the confidence, resilience, and opportunities they need to shape their future and make a positive impact on the world.
