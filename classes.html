Join the Havenridge Youth Centre Today!

Becoming a member of Havenridge Youth Centre is simple and completely free! As a member, you’ll have access to all our programs, events, and facilities designed to support and inspire young people.

To join, just visit us and speak with a staff member at reception. You’ll need to fill out a short membership form with some basic details so we can get to know you better. If you’re under 18, a parent or guardian will need to provide consent. Once registered, you’ll receive a membership card, giving you full access to everything we offer.

As a member, you’ll enjoy free programs, career support, and a welcoming space to connect with others. Join today and become part of a community that helps young people grow and thrive!
