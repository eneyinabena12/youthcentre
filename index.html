Welcome to the Havenridge Youth Centre

A place where young people can learn, grow, and thrive. At Havenridge, we offer a safe and supportive environment filled with exciting programs, mentorship opportunities, and a community that truly cares.

Whether you’re looking to develop new skills, meet new friends, or simply have a place to belong, we’re here for you.

Join us for fun activities, career workshops, mental health support, and more. This is your space—your future starts here!
